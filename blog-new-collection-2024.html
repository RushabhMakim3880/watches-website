New Arrivals

New Collection Launch 2024

Dec 8, 2024 4 min read By CHRONOLUX Team

We're thrilled to unveil our 2024 collection, a stunning fusion of traditional craftsmanship and cutting-edge innovation. This year's lineup represents our most ambitious effort yet, featuring groundbreaking designs that push the boundaries of horological excellence.

Highlights of the Collection

Our 2024 collection introduces several revolutionary features:

  • Advanced Materials: Incorporating titanium, ceramic, and carbon fiber for enhanced durability and lightweight comfort
  • Smart Integration: Hybrid models that blend mechanical precision with modern connectivity
  • Sustainable Craftsmanship: Eco-friendly materials and ethical sourcing practices
  • Enhanced Water Resistance: Up to 500m depth rating on select sport models

Featured Models

The Moonphase Prestige: Our flagship piece featuring an exquisite moonphase complication with exhibition caseback, showcasing the intricate automatic movement in all its glory.

Titanium Sport Chrono: A lightweight powerhouse combining chronograph functionality with a tachymeter scale, perfect for the modern athlete.

Smart Hybrid Connect: Bridging tradition and technology, this model offers fitness tracking and smartphone connectivity while maintaining classic aesthetics.

Innovation Meets Tradition

While embracing innovation, we remain committed to the timeless principles of watchmaking. Each piece in our 2024 collection undergoes rigorous quality control and is assembled by master craftsmen with decades of experience.

Limited Availability: Visit our showroom or browse online to explore the complete 2024 collection. Select models are available in limited quantities.
