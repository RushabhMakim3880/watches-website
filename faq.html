Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (1-2 days) and international shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination (typically 7-14 business days). Customs duties and taxes may apply.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order anytime on our order tracking page.

Returns & Exchanges

Can I exchange my watch?

Yes! Exchanges are available for different sizes, styles, or models within 30 days of purchase, subject to availability.

Product Information

Are your watches authentic?

100% guaranteed. All watches come with manufacturer certificates of authenticity and our own authenticity guarantee. Learn more on our authenticity page.

Do watches come with a warranty?

Yes! All watches include manufacturer warranties (typically 2-5 years depending on brand). See our warranty page for details.

How do I know which size watch to buy?

Most men's watches range from 38-44mm case diameter, while women's watches typically range from 28-38mm. We recommend visiting our showroom for sizing, or contact us for personalized recommendations.

Watch Care & Maintenance

How often should I service my watch?

We recommend professional servicing every 3-5 years for automatic watches, or when you notice accuracy issues. Quartz watches typically need battery replacement every 2-3 years.

Can I wear my watch while swimming?

It depends on the water resistance rating. Watches rated 50m or higher are suitable for swimming. Dive watches (200m+) are designed for scuba diving. Always ensure the crown is properly secured before water exposure.

Where can I get my watch serviced?

We offer in-house servicing by certified watchmakers. Contact us to schedule a service appointment or mail in your watch with our prepaid service kit.
