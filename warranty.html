Manufacturer Warranty

All TM WATCH watches come with comprehensive manufacturer warranties covering defects in materials and workmanship.

Warranty Coverage

  • TM WATCH Brand: 5-year international warranty
  • Partner Brands: 2-5 years depending on manufacturer
  • Automatic Movements: Full movement coverage
  • Quartz Movements: Movement and battery coverage

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in movement
  • Case and crystal defects
  • Strap or bracelet manufacturing defects
  • Water resistance failure (if properly maintained)

What's Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, strap wear)
  • Damage from accidents or misuse
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Battery replacement (quartz watches)
  • Cosmetic damage to case or crystal

Making a Warranty Claim

  1. Contact our service department at service@TM WATCH.com
  2. Provide proof of purchase and warranty card
  3. Describe the issue in detail
  4. Ship watch to our service center (prepaid label provided)
  5. Receive repaired watch within 2-3 weeks

Extended Warranty Available

Extend your coverage up to 10 years with our premium warranty program. Contact us for details.
