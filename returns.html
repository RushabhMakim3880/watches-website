Our Return Policy

We want you to love your TM WATCH timepiece. If you're not completely satisfied, we offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn watches in original condition.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Watch must be unworn and in pristine condition
  • All original packaging, tags, and documentation must be included
  • Return initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase required

How to Return Your Watch

  1. Contact Us: Email support@TM WATCH.com or call 1-800-CHRONO-X to initiate your return
  2. Receive Return Label: We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label
  3. Pack Securely: Place the watch in its original packaging with all accessories
  4. Ship: Drop off at any authorized shipping location
  5. Refund: Once received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

Want a different model or size? Exchanges are available within 30 days, subject to availability. Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Engraved or personalized watches
  • Watches showing signs of wear or damage
  • Final sale or clearance items

Need Help?

Our customer service team is here to assist with your return or exchange.
