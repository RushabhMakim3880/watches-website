Shipping Options

Standard Shipping (FREE on orders over $500)

Delivery in 3-5 business days within the continental US. $15 flat rate for orders under $500.

Express Shipping

Next-day or 2-day delivery available. Rates calculated at checkout based on location and order value.

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries. Delivery times vary by destination (typically 7-14 business days). Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same business day. Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends/holidays ship the next business day.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Track your shipment anytime on our order tracking page.
